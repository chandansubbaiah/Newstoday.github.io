till June 15 after meeting with Sports Minister:

Sports Minister Anurag Thakur speaks with the media after a meeting with wrestlers, outside his residence in New Delhi on June 7, 2023. June 15. “We were told that police investigation will be complete by June 15.
Till then, we have been asked to wait and suspend the protest,” Sakshi Malik told reporters after the meeting.
